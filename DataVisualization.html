Data Visualization

Visualize Environmental Pollution Data to

Plots

Through this interactive graph, you can visualize the correlation between the years and annual plastic production. This linear relationship demonstrates how as the years go by, the more and more plastic is being produced by mass factories.

In this interactive line graph, you can visualize the correlation between the years and CO2 Emissions Per Capita Data for the USA over the Years. Hover over the line graph to learn about the exact measurements of CO2 Emissions Per Capita over the years. Clearly, you can see that as the years go by, in general the CO2 Emissions increase. Compare the CO2 Emissions Per Capita Data in recent years to the years in the 1800s. Clearly, it's an unnatural increase.

In this bar graph, de-select and select either airquality or water pollution to toggle between data. Hover over the graph to learn more about air quality and water pollution indicies. The air quality varies from 0 (bad quality) to 100 (top good quality). The water pollution varies from 0 (no pollution) to 100 (top good quality).
